YT Deferred Uploader

Queue videos from Google Drive and upload them to YouTube — with quota awareness, automatic retries, and smart title generation.

How it works
  1. Connect your Google account
  2. Pick video files from Google Drive, or set up automatic folder polling
  3. Videos are queued and uploaded to YouTube on your behalf
Features
  • Smart title generation from video recording dates
  • Quota-aware uploads with automatic daily reset
  • Exponential backoff retry for transient failures
  • No video data stored — streams directly from Drive to YouTube
  • Auto-purge of completed job metadata
  • Full account deletion (right to erasure)
Your data

We request only the permissions needed to upload your selected videos. We never browse your Drive, access your playlists, or share your data with third parties. You can delete all your data at any time from Settings. Read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
